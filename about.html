ABOUT US

Refreshing Every Drop

At Liquifresh, we believe water is life and everyone deserves access to clean, safe, and refreshing water every day.
We are a trusted water supply company dedicated to providing households, businesses and communities with reliable water
delivery solutions that are pure, sustainable, and affordable.

Who We Are

Liquifresh was founded with a simple mission: to ensure that every sip you take is fresh, healthy and hydrating. Over the
years, we have grown into a dependable water supply partner, serving a wide range of customers from homes and offices to retail
outlets and large organizations.

What We Do

  • Water Delivery: Timely and reliable delivery of purified water straight to your door.
  • Bulk Supply: Safe and convenient water supply for businesses, events, and institutions.
  • Sustainable Practices: We use eco-friendly methods to minimize waste and protect the environment.

Established in 2023, Liquifresh is a small, business dedicated to selling purified bottled water and provides water refill solutions. The company was founded to address growing concerns about access to clean drinking water and reducing plastic waste. Liquifresh
operates both in store and via home deliveries. The company has built a positive reputation in the community for delivering high quality bottled water and promoting eco friendly refill stations.
